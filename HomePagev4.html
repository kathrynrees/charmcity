About This Blog

Welcome to my MVLA Web Design portfolio. Baltimore holds a special place in my heart so I wanted to create a list of my favorite places in Charm City to share with friends planning to visit Baltimore.

People call Baltimore "the city of neighborhoods" because it has more than 250 neighborhoods — each with its own distinct history and culture. Some Baltimore neighborhoods are large while others are only a few blocks wide. Throughout this blog, I've shared some ideas for neighborhoods to explore — and my favorite local parks, shops and restaurants within them (I'll also admit that having/lacking high quality photos did factor into some of my design decisions for this project).

The history of these neighborhoods is inextricably linked to Baltimore's history of racist housing policies and practices. Redlining, blockbusting and other racist policies have undoubtedly shaped the Baltimore we see today. Two books that unpack Baltimore's history, which I highly recommend, are: "The Black Butterfly" by Dr. Lawrence Brown and "Not in My Neighborhood" by Antero Pietila.
